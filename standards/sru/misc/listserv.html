SRU Listserv - "zng"

There is a public listserv for discussion of topics of interest to SRU implementors. Members of this listserv are the SRU Implementors Group (joining the listserv automatically makes you a member).

To join, send an email message to listserv@loc.gov  with the single line "subscribe zng" (the subject line for the message is irrelevant).

Once subscribed, to send a message, mail to zng@listserv.loc.gov.

To un-subscribe, send an email message to listserv@loc.gov  with the single line "signoff zng".

There is an archive at http://sun8.loc.gov/listarch/zng.html.
