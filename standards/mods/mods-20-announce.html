Announcement: MODS Version 2.0 Available for Use

The revised MODS schema version 2.0 is now available online at: lcnetdev.github.io/mods/mods.xsd.

Changes from the previous draft version 1.2 are documented at: http://lcnetdev.github.io/mods/changes.html.

The MODS outline and MARCXML to MODS stylesheet have been revised to reflect the new schema. The mappings and examples will be revised shortly.

The revision is based on input from implementors and from others who have participated in this list. Thank you all for contributing.
