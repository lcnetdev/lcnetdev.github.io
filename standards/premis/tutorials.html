How to sponsor a PREMIS tutorial

The PREMIS Editorial Committee will support additional PREMIS tutorials by providing instructors and training materials. Contact the PREMIS Managing Agency, the Library of Congress (premis at loc.gov).

Please note: Documents on this Web site are offered in several formats. To view PDF documents, a free viewer is available for download from Adobe Acrobat. Powerpoint presentations may either be viewed through your Web browser, or downloaded.
