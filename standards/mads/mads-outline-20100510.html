Outline of Elements and Attributes in MADS (rev. Apr. 26, 2010)

This document contains a listing of elements and their related attributes in the Metadata Authority Description Schema (MADS) with values or value sources where applicable. It is an "outline" of the schema.

The <authority> element is required and not repeatable. All other top-level elements are optional. There must be at least one subelement under <authority>. Multiple subelements may be used under <authority>. for instance a <name> and <titleInfo> or <topic> and <geographic>. Attributes are not in a mandated sequence and not repeatable (per XML rules).

"Authority" attributes are followed by references to documents that contain codes for identifying authority lists of controlled values.
