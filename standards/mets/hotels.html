METS Opening Day Hotel Information

Here are two possibilities for hotels that are nearby.
  • Capitol Hill Suites
    • 200 C Street SE
    • phone: 202/543-6000
    • fax: 202/547-2608

    The Capitol Hill Suites is just across the street from the Madison Building so is very convenient.


  • Holiday Inn Capitol Hill
    • 550 C street SW
    • phone: 202-479-4000

    The Holiday Inn Capitol Hill is not as close but is still walkable (about 3/4 mile). Note that the address is Southwest DC. The Library is in Southeast DC.

Of course there are many other possibilities. You might check www.hotelsdc.com for additional information.
