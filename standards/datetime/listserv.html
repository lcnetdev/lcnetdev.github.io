Listserv - "datetime"

There is a listserv for discussion the development of the EDTF Date/Time format.

To join, send an email message to listserv@loc.gov  with the single line "subscribe datetime " (the subject line for the message is irrelevant).

Once subscribed, to send a message, mail to datetime@loc.gov.

To un-subscribe, send an email message to listserv@loc.gov  with the single line "signoff datetime ".

There is an archive at http://sun8.loc.gov/listarch/datetime.html.
