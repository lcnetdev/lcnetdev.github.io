BIBFRAME Topics and Issues

On the BIBFRAME listserv there is interesting and valuable discussion on a wide variety of BIBFRAME topics. BIBFRAME Topics and Issues attempts to organize the discussion into a discrete list of entries, one for each topic, to describe the issues pertaining to the topic, to raise questions for further discussion, and to record conclusions, agreements, and remaining areas of disagreement.

Each topic entry will remain open and when there are new issues, conclusions, or areas of agreement or disagreement to report, the entry will be updated.
