BIBFRAME AV Assessment: Technical, Structural, and Administrative Metadata - September 23, 2015 (revised January 4, 2016)

This report presents the findings of a study conducted by AVPreserve on behalf of the Library of Congress to evaluate the existing state of technical, structural, and preservation metadata for audiovisual resources in the bibliographic environment in light of existing standards for audiovisual metadata, and to make recommendations about how BIBFRAME can support the expression of such information.
