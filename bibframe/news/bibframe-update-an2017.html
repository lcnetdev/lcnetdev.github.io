BIBFRAME Update Forum at ALA Annual Conference 2017

SUNDAY, June 25, 2017 - 10:30 - 11:30 am

McCormick Place S104

The BIBFRAME Update will focus on practical experience with BIBFRAME presenting information about the new Library of Congress Pilot that has just started this month, OCLC’s and LC’s issues and similarities in the creation of Work descriptions from MARC records, Stanford’s ambitious project to incorporate copy-cataloging into their Linked Data work stream, and Zepheira’s shift of BIBFRAME Lite to BIBFRAME in their implementations.

PRESENTATIONS

  • Library of Congress Pilot Two: more complete, more participants, more interactive (Powerpoint, 66 KB)
    Beacher Wiggins, Director for Acquisitions and Bibliographic Access, and Sally McCallum, Chief Network Development and Standards Office, Library of Congress 
  • BIBFRAME and OCLC Works: defining models and discovering evidence (Powerpoint, 5.17MB)
    Jean Godby, Senior Research Scientist, and Diane Vizine-Goetz, Senior Research Scientist, OCLC
  • LD4P Tracer Bullet 1: an RDF copy-cataloging pipeline (Powerpoint, 532 KB)
    Philip Schreur, Assistant University Librarian for Technical and Access Services, Stanford University
  • Transformation, BIBFRAME, and the Library.Link Network (Powerpoint, 9.98 MB)
    Eric Miller, President, Zepheira
