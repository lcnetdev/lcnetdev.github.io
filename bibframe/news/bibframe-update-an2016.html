BIBFRAME Update Forum at the 2016 ALA Annual Conference

SUNDAY, June 26, 10:30 am - 12:00 pm

Orange County Convention Center, Room W208 (Orlando, Florida)

The BIBFRAME Update at ALA will feature four projects that are being built around BIBFRAME, the Library of Congress Pilot, the LD4P grant and project, the Libhub initiative, and OCLC’s data collaborations.
